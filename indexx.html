About Us

We are a local family owned business since our inception in 2004. We at Amalka Flora specialize in all kinds of wedding flowers, gift flowers, corporate flowers, home decor and sympathy flower arrangements and delivery.
Amalka Flora is a one stop shop for all your floral requirements from a simple buttonhole flower to an elegant church decoration or a wedding venue decoration with beautiful and stylish designs.
Our business has expanded to five locations in Dalugama, Wattala, Mabola, Ja ela and Gampaha to cater you better and make flower arrangements more accessible to everyone.
Our success over the years are coming from the sustained quality of the products and services which are offered at affordable prices.
